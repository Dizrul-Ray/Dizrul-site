About

Tech should feel simple, and personal.

I built Dizrul’s Digital Management to be the tech department small businesses wish they had: responsive, transparent, and invested in long-term wins.

Why I do this

Tech is my passion, and I love staying up to date, especially with how fast AI and new tools are changing business. I’ve also seen the struggle: a good small business doing great work, but losing opportunities because a few tech pieces aren’t in place.

I’m a married father of four and a proud grandpa of two. I care about family, hard work, and local businesses. My goal is to help owners compete in this evolving world without paying an arm and a leg.

  • Human support: you’ll work with a real person who knows your business.
  • Transparency: clear scope, clear costs, and no mystery jargon.
  • Problem-solving: fix what’s broken, then build systems that don’t keep breaking.
  • Value first: prioritize the changes that actually make a real difference.

How I help

Some businesses need tech support. Some need better visibility. Most need both. I combine helpdesk-style support with website and GBP work so your business stays reachable and easy to choose.
