## Project
This is the marketing website for Dizrul's Digital Management.

## Brand positioning
Dizrul's Digital Management is the human tech department for small businesses and solo startups.

## Audience
Small business owners and one-person startups who want help with websites, Google presence, tech setup, and simple automation without confusing jargon.

## Core services
- website design
- website hosting help
- Google Business Profile management
- small business tech support
- simple AI integration

## Voice and tone
- plain English
- trustworthy
- helpful
- human
- practical
- never overly corporate
- never jargon-heavy

## Rules
- keep the site simple and easy to maintain
- prefer improving existing code over unnecessary rewrites
- preserve compatibility with Cloudflare Pages
- do not break working forms, links, or layout
- keep mobile responsiveness strong
- flag duplicate or outdated pages before deleting them
- check for typos, broken links, CTA clarity, and conversion issues before finishing

## Business goals
- make the homepage clearer
- improve trust
- improve lead generation
- unify the offer path around Free Evaluation -> Launch Sprint -> Monthly Support
